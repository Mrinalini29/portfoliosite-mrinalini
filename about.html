About

My Story

Hi! I am Mrinalini, currently based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I am a homemaker, and while I do not have prior experience in UX or design, I have an artistic background. I create DIY crafts, home projects, and some commissioned artwork. I have now embarked on a journey to learn UX Design, with a focus on front-end development. Being a creative individual enhances my attention to detail and organizational skills, and my experience with commissioned artwork has taught me the importance of customer satisfaction. Throughout my UX journey with Career Foundry, I have learned a great deal about research and interview skills. I have also gained knowledge in using Figma, Canva, and other tools to create highly intuitive and seamless user experiences. I am eager to collaborate with other UX designers to expand my expertise and contribute to creating exceptional user experiences.

When I'm not learning, I enjoy creating mandala art and exploring other forms of art, such as texture art and resin crafts, as well as various DIY projects. My partner and I love to travel, and since moving here, we've visited over 45 national parks in the U.S. I also like to make short videos of the places we visit and engage in nature photography. I cherish every moment I spend outdoors and love capturing the beauty of nature for memories.

I am eager to apply my skills and creativity to a team that prioritizes growth and user-centered design.
