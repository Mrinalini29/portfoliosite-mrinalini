My Work

Olive

Olive is an online platform dedicated to health and well-being, designed to help users maintain and improve their physical and mental health all in one place. In today's fast-paced world, many individuals struggle to find a balance between work and health, often neglecting their health needs.

While there are numerous health and wellness platforms available, many lack inclusivity and fail to provide a positive user experience. This project aims to create a wellness portal that is intuitive, inclusive, and thoughtful, ensuring that users have a good experience while making their health and wellness journey simpler and more effective.
