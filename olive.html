OLIVE-Health & Wellness App

What is OLIVE?

Olive is an online platform dedicated to health and well-being, designed to help users maintain and improve their physical and mental health all in one place. In today's fast-paced world, many individuals struggle to find a balance between work and health, often neglecting their health needs. While there are numerous health and wellness platforms available, many lack inclusivity and fail to provide a positive user experience. This project aims to create a wellness portal that is intuitive, inclusive, and thoughtful, ensuring that users have a good experience while making their health and wellness journey simpler and more effective.

Possible Problems

  • Privacy- User privacy must be our first priority, as this appears to be a possible problem we must ensure that users' privacy is protected at all times.
  • Generic- Information Generic information may not be suitable for individuals with specific medical histories who require personalized guidance.
  • Inclusivity- The platform might not be inclusive of physical, psychological, and social needs of potential users.
  • Costs- There might be charges for specific advice to the users, and the costs could be high.
  • Overwhelming- So many features might be overwhelming for some users without proper guidance.

Problem Statement

After utilising the double diamond strategy and coming up with possible problems a user might face while using various health and wellness apps, I came up with the problem statement. "Health-conscious individuals need a responsive web application that provides wellness and health information as well as stores their medical data securely because they need a one stop place for all their health and wellness requirements which they can access on the go. We will know this to be true when we observe individual users receiving support, monitoring their progress, and developing healthy habits through the use of our application."

Potential Solutions

  • Provide users with a secure platform where they can securely store their health details.
  • Develop a cost-effective pricing model allowing users to access tailored advice while ensuring basic platform features are available to all.
  • Provide platform tutorials for easy navigation.
  • Design a user-friendly UI for diverse users.

Competitive Analysis

After identifying potential problems and solutions, I conducted a competitive analysis of existing apps and platforms that are leading in the health and wellness market. The SWOT analysis greatly helped shape my vision for OLIVE, which aims to create a more inclusive and user-friendly app. Below, I will present the SWOT analysis for the two leading apps.

User Interviews

After conducting the competitive analysis and gathering the insights I designed some research goals like what the users actually needed from a wellness app and how could a wellness app make their life easier. Based on the research goals I conducted User interviews and collected feedback which shaped the direction of the app and helped me focus on features that would actaully help the users.

Research Goals

  • Understand user needs: What do the users need in a health app? How often the users need expert advice? The need of a health app in their day to day life, etc.
  • Understand user motivation: What motivates the users to use a health app? What are the goals of the users?
  • Understand user behavior: How do the users keep track of their health on everyday basis?
  • Understand user pain points: What problems the users face with their health ? What overwhelms or frustrates them while using a health app?

A fully realized design system includes basic elements such as colors, shapes, lines, and contrast, as well as more complex components like platform patterns, animations, iconography, sizing, gestures, and overall graphical and visual design styles. Essentially, it serves as a central repository for all common design and UX patterns related to the product.

User Testing

After creating high-fidelity prototypes for the OLIVE app, remote user testing was conducted. A test plan was implemented where the target audience was asked to perform specific tasks using the app. Feedback from the users was then collected, and necessary corrections were made based on their insights. The app is still a work in progress, with the single goal of providing users with an inclusive and smooth user experience.

Reflection

The creation of OLIVE involved testing at every stage of development. After producing high-fidelity prototypes, we conducted user testing to gather valuable insights, which informed further refinements. I am dedicated to designing for a diverse range of users, ensuring a friendly and positive user experience.
